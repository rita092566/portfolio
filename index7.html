SDGs Manual

Plan b Inc. proposed sustainable development (SD) as "New Tools". With this new working language, we propose various substantive solutions with different thinking modes for various life problems. However, due to the insufficient introduction of Chinese on sustainable development and the limited understanding of the public's sustainable development goals, the "SDGs Manual" was specially compiled. Demonstrate how "Social/Issue" and "Business Strategy" can be combined to solve problems through international cases.

I was responsible for collecting data on the SDGs Taiwan Shadow Report, analyzing the current situation of Taiwan for 17 SDGs, and compiling the results into a simple chart. This project was a big challenge for me at the beginning. I had to digest a lot of government data and summarize it into valuable data. After about half a year, I not only learned the method of summarizing data but also had a deeper understanding of Taiwan's economic and environmental issues. For example, I have researched many issues of migrant workers in Southeast Asia, and I made new friends by interviewing the One-Forty team. Since then, I have learned more about their perspectives and culture.
