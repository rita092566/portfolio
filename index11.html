Interior Design

CHUCK SU

✱ Ongoing construction ✱
CHUCK SU is a photo studio in Kaohsiung that the client commissioned us to design the interior design of the second store. This store has only 85m2, and the primary purpose is to take ID photos, and the owner hopes to create a sense of technology and the future. The design that I am most satisfied with is that the facade has three viewing windows, which represent the camera's lens, and the images inside cooperate with the graphic designer and are expected to be changed regularly. The brand blue dominates the interior, the counter is made of a double-layer mirror as the surface material, and the clothes storage cabinet is made of blue glass as the frame.
