Alife

I was the PM of this project and one of the earliest members, responsible for branding from 0 to 1. Including brand name, slogan, rental space model, software service, official website, and evaluation of how to cooperate with government or enterprises to renovate existing real estate. It took about two years from research to a product model. The biggest challenge is that it will take many years for the renovation to be profitable, and it takes work to gain investors' trust. After constantly revising the product model and financial evaluation, we finally got our first building in Shilin District. At present, Alife has two bases in Taipei and nearly 200 members. Although I have left the company, I still cooperate with the team to do space merchandising at each location.
