Alife

Alife is a service provider for a variety of life style solutions. We want to enable everyone to enjoy the life style they want through multiple forms of living spaces (residential, industrial, work, etc) and life style services. Alife does not take anything for granted, our lifestyle projects, whether in concept or space usages combination, break the boundaries. Through different dimensions of co-branding, collaborations, and product developments, Alife takes the members of our community to expand their lives to the entire city. At the same time, Alife brings the most interesting stories in the city to their homes.

I was responsible for branding from 0 to 1. Including brand name, slogan, rental space model, software service, official website, and evaluation of how to cooperate with government or enterprises to renovate existing real estate. It took about two years from research to a product model. The biggest challenge is that it will take many years for the renovation to be profitable, and it takes work to gain investors' trust. After constantly revising the product model and financial evaluation, we finally got our first building in Shilin District. At present, Alife has two bases in Taipei and nearly 200 members. Although I have left the company, I still cooperate with the team to do visual merchandising at each location.
