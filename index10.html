Little Tree Food

✱ Ongoing construction ✱
Little Tree Food is a vegetarian restaurant awarded the Taipei Michelin Green Star in 2022. This store is the third branch of the brand, located in Diamond Tower, a department store in the Eastern District of Taipei, and is expected to open in 2023. The store's design is based on the concept of a farm, extensively using natural colors and original materials, such as recycled terracotta bricks, cement, and mineral paints. The ceiling uses hanging plants with bulb lighting to create outdoor vibes. The wine cabinet behind the bar is designed with the image of a nest, and the material of the cement bricks on the bar's facade is extended to maintain consistency.
