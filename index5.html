Coworking Space

CIT

Office space is, in essence, the place for mental work; the mental development of workers is thus influenced by the office space where they stay. Therefore, the way to ignite a revolution in modern society is to redefine office spaces where modern people spend most of their time working. We believe: “office space would consistently evolve with the upgrading of the economy”. We define CIT as an innovative office space to stimulate the curiosity of members or participants, to catalyze the interactions among communities of enterprises, startups and freelancers, and therefore to reach the goals of sustainable development in Taiwan.

Here's what I did in this project: I am responsible for the planning of public spaces and meeting rooms, as well as the formulation of SOP for operating procedures. There are three meeting rooms, each with different functions and themes inspired by the movie The Matrix. The small conference room is named Morpheus, mainly for the startup team to brainstorm, so the whole wall is planned to be a blackboard. The medium conference room is named Trinity, a billiard table replaces the conference table, and the large conference room is set to the general conference room specification. In the early stage of operation, I observed the operation status and recorded daily analysis to formulate a complete SOP, thinking about how to simplify the process and achieve effective operation. After three months of trial operation, the SOP can be easily used by all personnel.
