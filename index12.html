Draft Land

Draft Land is a cocktail brand created by Drinks Lab. It carries out the brand spirit of “to be free” through the offering of everyday’s cocktails. The Draft Land team crafts a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails with its Vacuum Distillery System by injecting nitrous oxide (N20) or carbon dioxide (CO2) into beverages to fast create high quality, sustainable, affordable selections of specialty cocktails. In 2018, the first brand store opened in Taipei, with anticipation of expansion into other Asian cities by the end of the year. With the initiation of its bottled cocktail development, it will increase its accessibility in different lifestyle markets, becoming the industry's leading cocktail brand.

Before the opening of Draft Land's first store, I was responsible for branding, including designing an official website and running a social media account. The official website is set up with Wix template, and my colleagues and I edit the shooting and graphic materials. In the initial social media, we made two projects to convey the brand spirit. The first is a dictionary dedicated to the brand, which defines the meaning of words by itself. The second is to create an image through wine glasses and elements of daily life. In addition, I was also responsible for the VIP opening party of the Taipei store and Hong Kong store, including electronic invitations and event rundown.
